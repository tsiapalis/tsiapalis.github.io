About Us

"As the brainchild of game developer Antonis Tsiapalis, our studio represents a fusion of individual creativity and collaborative synergy. While I lead the charge as a solo developer, I'm fortunate to have a talented network of collaborators who contribute their expertise in various capacities. Partnering with Stavros Markonis and his team, a seasoned music composer from the film industry, we breathe life into our worlds with evocative soundscapes. Additionally, Kostas Moschopoulos, an avid gamer and theory crafter, lends his talents as a narrative and level designer. At Steps Game Studio, our ethos centers on harnessing the collective strengths of our team, ensuring that each project is imbued with passion, innovation, and artistic flair. With our singular focus on our flagship project, we strive to craft an immersive gaming experience that resonates with players worldwide."
